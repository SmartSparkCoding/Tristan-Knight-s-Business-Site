General Information

Everything you need to know before booking a session.

Natural light, clean composition, and a calm working style keep every session easy and comfortable. Edited galleries are usually delivered within 7 to 10 business days, depending on the scope of the shoot.

Portraits, lifestyle, events, and custom commissions are all available with flexible planning and location options. I work closely with clients to shape the mood, composition, and final look of each gallery so the images feel intentional and polished.
